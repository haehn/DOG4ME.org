Evaluation Criteria

  • Public Access Test compliance (calm behavior in public, non-aggressive, no barking)
  • Basic Obedience (sit, stay, down, heel, recall)
  • Task-Specific Skills:
    • Deep Pressure Therapy
    • Medical Alert
    • Mobility Assistance
  • Handler control and team communication
  • Reaction to distractions and startle recovery
  • Behavior around other animals and people
